HRT Stats

Track your transition without turning your life into a spreadsheet.

A private app for logging hormone therapy doses, lab results, check-ins, and appointment notes. Your health notes stay on your device unless you choose to export them.

Privacy Support

Built for notes, not medical advice.

HRT Stats helps you organize information, notice patterns, and prepare better questions for a clinician. It does not diagnose, prescribe, interpret labs as safe or unsafe, or recommend medication changes.

Dose history

Log medication, amount, route, timing, and notes so your current regimen is easier to review.

Lab organization

Save hormone-related lab values with blood draw timing and plain-English context.

Check-ins

Track symptoms, body changes, mood, sleep, libido, and side effects over time.

Appointment prep

Turn recent notes into a concise summary you can copy, export, or bring to a visit.

Privacy Policy

Your data stays on your device.

Last updated: May 13, 2026

HRT Stats is designed as a local-first personal health notebook. The app does not require an account, does not sell personal data, does not use third-party advertising, and does not track you across apps or websites.

Information you enter

You may choose to enter doses, lab values, check-ins, symptoms, notes, appointment questions, and related health information. This information is stored locally on your device. It is not uploaded to HRT Stats servers.

Exports and imports

If you export, copy, import, or share data, that action is controlled by you and may involve apps or services outside HRT Stats, such as Files, Mail, Messages, or cloud storage providers you choose.

Lab image scanning

If you use image or document import features, they are intended to help extract values into your own notes. HRT Stats does not use those values to diagnose, prescribe, or recommend medication changes.

Deleting data

You can delete data from inside the app. Removing the app from your device may also remove locally stored app data, depending on your device settings and backups.
