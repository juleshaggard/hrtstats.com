Support

Need help with HRT Stats?

Email support questions, bug reports, or feature requests. Please do not send emergency or urgent medical questions.

Contact

Email support@hrtstats.com.

What to include

If you are reporting a bug, include your device model, iOS version, app version, and what you were trying to do.

Medical questions

HRT Stats helps organize notes and lab values, but it does not provide medical advice. For questions about symptoms, lab interpretation, medication, or dosing, contact a qualified clinician.

Privacy
